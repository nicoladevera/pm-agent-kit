## Launch Types

<!-- How your company categorizes launches. The four standard types:

- **Internal**: Feature available to internal users or team only. Testing, dogfooding, internal validation. Minimal external communication.
- **Alpha**: Limited external release to a hand-selected group. Gathering early signal. Explicit opt-in, known users, direct feedback channels.
- **Beta**: Broader external release with known limitations. May be feature-flagged or percentage-rolled. Users may self-enroll or be invited. Public-facing but not fully polished.
- **GA (General Availability)**: Full production release to all users. Complete documentation, support enablement, monitoring, and rollback plan. The feature is "shipped."

- Who approves (PM only? PM + engineering lead? VP sign-off?)
- What communication is expected (internal Slack? Changelog? Blog post? Email campaign?)
- What documentation is required (release notes? Help center article? Sales enablement?)
- What monitoring must be in place before launch
