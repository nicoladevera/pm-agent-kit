# Business Case Input — Best Price Guarantee

Hey, I want to build a business case for a Best Price Guarantee feature. Here's my thinking.

---

## The Idea

We should build a Best Price Guarantee: if a customer finds the same pro offering the same service at a lower price directly (off-platform), we'll match it. This keeps bookings on the platform instead of bleeding to direct relationships.

The way it would work: customer contacts support with proof (a screenshot or text of the direct quote), we verify it's the same pro + same service, and we honor the matched price. The customer pays the matched amount and we absorb the difference between our platform price and the match price.

---

## Context

We're a two-sided marketplace for home services — customers book, pros provide. The numbers:

- Platform GMV is $24M/year
- We charge an 18% take rate on all bookings
- 23% of our bookings are repeat customers (customers who've booked at least twice)
- Our most recent churn survey asked non-returning customers why they didn't book again — "found the same pro cheaper directly" came in as the #3 reason for non-return, cited by 31% of non-returning customers who completed the survey
- Average booking value: $185

We know customers are finding our pros on the platform first and then going around us to book directly at a lower price. This is classic marketplace leakage — the platform is essentially acting as a lead generation service for the pros rather than a transaction layer.

---

## Why This Is the Right Call

I think this is clearly the right call. The #3 churn reason is price leakage, and the guarantee directly addresses it. We also believe it signals to customers that we're confident in our pricing and committed to their experience — which should improve trust.

Alternatives? We could just not do this, but that doesn't solve the leakage problem. The leakage will continue eating into our repeat booking rate.
