# One-Pager Input — Internal Hackathon Proposal

I want to pitch an internal hackathon to my VP of Engineering. Need her to approve it and allocate some budget.

---

## The Idea

We should run a 2-day internal hackathon next quarter. I think it would be really good for the team. A few reasons:

- Morale has been low since the reorg — people are heads-down on sprint work and haven't had time to explore ideas
- We keep talking about innovation in all-hands but haven't done anything concrete about it
- Two engineers on my team have side projects they've been tinkering with on weekends that could actually become product features
- I saw that Shopify and Stripe both run internal hackathons and it seems to work well for them

---

## What I'm Thinking

- 2-day event, maybe a Thursday-Friday so it doesn't kill a full sprint
- Teams of 3-5 form around ideas they pitch on day one morning
- Demo and judging at end of day two
- Some kind of prizes — maybe extra PTO days or a small cash prize
- Could invite the product and design teams too, not just engineering

---

## Concerns

- My VP is very delivery-focused — she'll want to know the ROI and won't love the idea of "losing" two days of sprint velocity
- We've never done this before so there's no precedent
- Budget: we'd need food/drinks for ~40 people, maybe some swag, and the prizes. I'm guessing $3-5K total?
- I'm not sure if this is the right time — we have a big launch coming in 6 weeks

---

## What I Need

Can you turn this into a one-pager I can put in front of my VP? She's the approver and budget holder. I need her to say yes and allocate the budget.
